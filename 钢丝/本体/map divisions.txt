The '''map''' is where most tactical and strategic decisions happen. It also provides information related to these purposes, such as, [[weather]], [[air combat]], [[land combat]], [[naval combat]], and [[buildings]].

== Map divisions ==
{{SVersion|1.9}}
The Map can be divided into four subdivisions:

* '''[[Province]]s''' are the smallest subunit of the map, it is here that [[terrain]], [[weather]], [[capital]]s, [[land combat]] and victory points are handled.

* '''[[State]]s''' are the next smallest subunit of the map, where [[construction]], [[manpower]], population, and country borders are decided.

* '''[[Supply area]]s''' are the next largest subunit of the map, here logistics and supply are handled.

* '''[[Strategic Region]]s''' are the largest of the subdivisions, and are where [[naval combat]] and [[air combat]] occur.

== Map modes ==
[[File:Mapmodes.png|300px|thumb|right|The map mode icons.]]
Using different '''map modes''', you can alter the information presented on the map.  These map modes can be activated by either clicking on the appropriate button (icon) located in the lower right corner of the main screen or by pressing the associated [[Hotkeys#Map mode interface hotkeys|hotkey]].  

=== 默认地图模式 ===
[[File:Default map mode button.png|left]]
[[File:Default map nogui 1.4.png|300px|thumb|right|The Default map mode without the day/night loop.]]
Shows sovereign nations and armies.

The default map mode is a combination of the political map mode and terrain map mode from previous games. It shows the name of all the nations, with their political color. Zoomed out, mainly the nation's color is seen, however, when zooming in, the terrain becomes more and more visible, integrating both map modes into one. Air fields, naval bases, capitals, and almost any other object on the map can be seen in this mode, represented by different symbols: 
*'''Naval bases''' are anchors.
*'''Airfields''' are a top down picture of an airplane.
*'''Capitals''' are a star, which can be {{green|green}} if it is the nation's capital, '''gray''' if they are any other nation's capital, or {{red|red}} if they are the capital of any of the nation's enemies.
*'''Victory points''' are denoted by a small dot, which have a name when zoomed in, and particularly high value victory points are denoted by a slightly larger dot. They follow the color scheme of capitals.
*'''Units''' can be seen as counters or sprites on the map.
*[[Battles|'''Battles''']] can be seen as pointers that are {{green|green}} if the nation is winning the battle, '''yellow''' if the battle is favoring neither side, or {{red|red}} if the nation is losing the battle, and it shows a number that summarizes how much the battle favors one side or the other.
*[[Naval combat|'''Naval combat''']] can be seen along with navies and their missions.
*'''Aircraft''' can be seen flying where aircraft are in operation.
*'''Forts''' and any other state- or province-wide constructions are also visible.
*'''Demilitarized zones''' will be a {{red|red}} outline around any demilitarized area, such as the Rhineland.
{{clear}}

=== Strategic navy map mode ===
{{SVersion|1.9}}
[[File:Naval map nogui.png|300px|thumb|right|The Strategic Navy map mode.]]
[[File:Strategic Navy map mode button.png|left]]
Shows strategic regions relevant to naval activity, with information on naval supremacy and naval tasks.

The naval map mode consists of the world's strategic regions, with a color scheme similar to the diplomacy for states on land under it. Convoy routes are also shown here with the following color scheme:

*{{blue|Blue}} trade routes are resource transfers. These are imports, exports or resource transfers from owned oversea resources.
*'''White''' trade routes are supply transfers to oversea units.
*{{green|Green}} trade routes are lend lease routes.

Trade routes require [[convoys]] to operate.

Strategic regions that have sea provinces will be highlighted with {{green|green}} if they are under allied naval superiority. Provinces that are striped {{red|red}} are out of the fleets' operational range.

==== Straits ====
[[File:Strait canal icon.png|left]] Canals or any type of controlled strait are shown on this map mode, and by hovering over them it is possible to see the access permissions your country has as well as the land provinces controlling the strait (blue outline). Straits are also shown on the Supply Map Mode and the Resource Map mode.

The following, are the names and province control points of the straits/canals on map and their respective permissions granted to nations (depending on the relations with the strait controllers).

=== Strategic air map mode ===
{{SVersion|1.9}}
[[File:Air map nogui.png|300px|thumb|right|The Strategic Air map mode.]]
[[File:Strategic Air map mode button.png|left]]
Shows strategic regions with information on aerial supremacy. It is automatically activated when an air base is selected.

The air map mode is, at first glance, merely a map of the strategic regions of the map. However, when at war, the regions can be highlighted with three colors:
*{{red|Red}}, which means the enemy has air superiority.
*{{green|Green}}, which means the nation or its allies has air superiority.
*<span style="color: orange;">'''Yellow'''</span>, which means the air superiority is contested.

This map mode also shows all of the air missions currently set with the total number of planes assigned. <br>
In the strategic air map mode [[File:More Ground Crews.png|30px]] more ground crews can be assigned, by clicking the icon in the desired strategic region. This gives a {{green|+10%}} bonus to mission efficiency in the strategic region. 
{{clear}}

=== Terrain map mode ===
{{SVersion|1.9}}
[[File:Land Terrain Map.png|300px|thumb|right|Land terrain in terrain mapmode.]]
[[File:Navalterrains.png|300px|thumb|right|Naval terrain in terrain mapmode.]]
The terrain map mode shows the [[terrain]] of each region on the map using simplified colors. Land terrain has high adjusters for attack, defense and movement of land units. The terrain map mode can be useful to plan your offense or defense in for you favorable terrain.

Colors of the terrains:
* '''Plains''': Brown 
* '''Forest''': Green
* '''Jungle''': Green 
* '''Marsh''': Green/blue
* '''Hills''': Light grayish with a bit yellow or green
* '''Mountain''': Gray
* '''Urban''': Dark Gray with road patterns
* '''Desert''': Yellow 	
* '''Ocean''': Blue
* '''Deep ocean''': Dark blue
* '''Shallow sea''': Light blue 			
* '''Fjords and archipelagos''': Cyan
{{clear}}

=== Resistance map mode===
{{SVersion|1.9}}
[[File:Resistance map nogui.png|300px|thumb|right|Resistance map mode.]]
The resistance map mode shows [[resistance]] per state. Yellow indicates low, orange higher and red high resistance.<br>
The percentage of resistance (0%-100%) is shown for each state. A green arrow downwards shows decreasing resistance, a red arrow upwards increasing resistance.<br>
Also 3 resistance tiers are shown:
* [[File:Resistance organized resistance.png]] '''Organized Resistance''' if the resistance is {{red| >25% }}
* [[File:Resistance emboldened resistance.png]] '''Emboldened Resistance''' if the resistance is {{red| >50% }}
* [[File:Resistance uprising.png]] '''Uprising''' if the resistance is {{red| >75% }}
{{clear}}

=== Compliance map mode===
{{SVersion|1.9}}
[[File:Compliance map.png|300px|thumb|right|Compliance map mode.]]
Shows [[compliance]] per state. 
Lighter blue means more compliance. <br>
The percentage of compliance (0%-100%) is shown for each state. <br>
Also 5 compliance tiers are shown:
* [[File:Compliance informants.png]] '''Informants''' if the compliance is {{green| >15% }}
* [[File:Compliance local police force.png]] '''Local Police Force''' if the compliance is {{green| >25% }}
* [[File:Compliance reorganized workforce.png]] '''Reorganized Workforce''' if the compliance is {{green| >40% }}
* [[File:Compliance volunteer program.png]] '''Volunteer Program''' if the compliance is {{green| >60% }}
* [[File:Compliance a new regime.png]] '''A New Regime''' if the compliance is {{green| >80% }}
{{clear}}

=== Resource map mode ===
{{SVersion|1.9}}
[[File:Resource map nogui.png|300px|thumb|right|The Resource map mode.]]
Shows [[resource]] locations and trade routes.

The resources map mode shows any convoy/ trade routes that are connected to the nation and canals/ straits across the world, similar to the naval map mode. However, this map mode looks like the default map mode when zoomed out, but when zoomed in, it shows a resource icon above any state that has a resource, along with the number of that resource produced in that province.
{{clear}}

=== Diplomacy map mode ===
{{SVersion|1.9}}
[[File:Diplomacy map nogui.png|300px|thumb|right|The Diplomacy map mode.]]
Shows [[Diplomacy|diplomatic]] relations.

The diplomacy map mode shows its information through a multitude of colors.
*{{green|Green}} is the color that any land owned and controlled by the nation 
*{{green|Light green}} is the color that any land occupied by the nation will be colored.
*{{green|Dark green}} is the color of any nation that has very good relations with the nation.
*{{red|Red}} is the color of any nations that the nation is currently at war with.
*{{red|Light red}} is the color of friendly territory that is occupied by enemies.
*'''Neon orange''' is the color of countries that the nation has [[wargoals]] against.
*'''Light orange''' is the color of any country the nation has very bad relations with.
*{{blue|Blue}} is the color of any allied nations.
{{clear}}

=== Factions map mode ===
{{SVersion|1.9}}
[[File:Faction map nogui.png|300px|thumb|right|The Factions map mode.]]
The [[factions]] map mode shows the factions that exist around the world. The faction is colored the color of the leader nation, i.e. the Axis is grey due to the color of Germany, and it stretches across all nations in that faction.
{{clear}}

=== States map mode ===
{{SVersion|1.9}}
[[File:States map nogui.png|300px|thumb|right|The States map mode.]]
Shows [[states]] and population/ state category.

The states map mode shows an outline of every state in the world. These outlines are different colors based on the population of the state/ state category. This directly controls how many factories are allowed to be built in a state. The type of state can be found by simply hovering over it.
{{clear}}

'''地图''是大多数战术和战略决策发生的地方。它还提供与这些目的相关的信息，如[[天气]]、[[空战]]、[[陆战]]、[[海战]]和[[建筑]]。

== 地图划分 ==
{{SVersion|1.9}}。
地图可以分为四个子单元。

* '''[[省]]'是地图最小的子单位，这里处理[[地形]]、[[天气]]、[[首都]]、[[陆战]]和胜利点。

* '''[[国家]]'是地图的下一个最小的子单元，在这里决定[[建筑]]、[[[人力]]]、人口和国家边界。

* '''[[供应区]]''是地图的下一个最大的子单位，这里处理物流和供应。

* '''[[战略区域]]''是最大的子单元，是[[海战]]和[[空战]]发生的地方。

== 地图模式 ==
[[File:Mapmodes.png|300px|thumb|right|地图模式图标]]。
使用不同的'''地图模式'''，你可以改变地图上的信息。 这些地图模式可以通过点击位于主屏幕右下角的相应按钮（图标）或按下相关的[[热键#地图模式界面热键|热键]]来激活。 

=== 默认地图模式 ===
[[文件:默认地图模式按钮.png|left]] 。
[[File:Default map nogui 1.4.png|300px|thumb|right|Default map mode without the day/night loop.]] 。
显示主权国家和军队。

默认地图模式是以前游戏中政治地图模式和地形地图模式的结合。它显示的是所有国家的名称，以及他们的政治颜色。放大后，主要看到的是国家的颜色，然而放大后，地形会越来越明显，将两种地图模式融为一体。机场、海军基地、首都等几乎所有地图上的物体都可以在这个模式下看到，用不同的符号表示。
*海军基地是锚。
*''''机场''是飞机的俯视图。
*''''首都'''是一颗星，如果是国家的首都，可以是{{绿色|绿色}}，如果是其他国家的首都，可以是'''灰色''，如果是国家敌人的首都，可以是{{红色|红色}}。
*'''胜利点'''用一个小点表示，放大后有一个名称，特别高价值的胜利点用一个稍大的点表示。它们遵循首都的颜色方案。
*''''单位''''可以在地图上以计数器或精灵的形式出现。
*[[战役|'''战役''']]可以看作是指针，如果国家在战斗中获胜，则为{{绿色|绿色}}，如果战斗对双方都不利，则为'''黄色'''，如果国家在战斗中失败，则为{{红色|红色}}，它显示一个数字，总结战斗对一方或另一方的有利程度。
*[[海军作战|'''海军作战''']]可以看到海军及其任务。
*''''飞机'''可以看到在飞机运行的地方飞行。
*''''要塞'''以及其他任何国家或省份范围内的建筑也可以看到。
*''''非军事区'''在任何非军事区周围都会有一个{{红色|red}}的轮廓，例如莱茵地区。
{{清晰}}。

===战略海军地图模式 ===
{{SVersion|1.9}}。
[[File:Naval map nogui.png|300px|thumb|right|战略海军地图模式]]。
[[File:Strategic Navy map mode button.png|left]] 。
显示与海军活动相关的战略区域，并提供海军霸主和海军任务的信息。

海军地图模式由世界战略区域组成，其颜色方案类似于其下的陆地上国家的外交。航行路线也在这里显示，配色方案如下。

*{{蓝色|蓝色}}贸易路线是资源转移。这些是拥有的海外资源的进口、出口或资源转移。
*''''白色''贸易路线是对海外单位的供应转移。
*{{绿|绿}}贸易路线是借贷租赁路线。

贸易路线需要[[船队]]来运作。

拥有海上省份的战略区域，如果它们处于盟军海军优势之下，则会以{{绿色|绿色}}高亮显示。有{{红色|红色}}条纹的省份不在舰队的作战范围内。

==== 海峡 ====
[[文件:海峡运河图标.png|left]] 运河或任何类型的控制海峡都会显示在这个地图模式上，将鼠标悬停在它们上面，可以看到你的国家拥有的通行权限以及控制海峡的陆地省份（蓝色轮廓）。海峡在补给地图模式和资源地图模式中也有显示。

以下，是地图上海峡/运河的名称和省份控制点，以及它们各自授予国家的权限（取决于与海峡控制者的关系）。

===战略空中地图模式 ===
{{SVersion|1.9}}。
[[File:Air map nogui.png|300px|thumb|right|The Strategic Air map mode.]]。
[[File:Strategic Air map mode button.png|left]]。
显示具有空中优势信息的战略区域。当选择一个空军基地时，它会被自动激活。

空中地图模式，乍一看只是地图上的战略区域的地图。然而，在战争中，这些地区可以用三种颜色突出显示。
*{{红|红}}，表示敌人拥有空中优势。
*{{绿|绿}}，表示国家或其盟友拥有空中优势。
*<span style="color: orange;">''Yellow''</span>，这意味着空中优势有争议。

该地图模式还显示了当前设置的所有空中任务，以及分配的飞机总数。<br>
在战略空军地图模式中，[[File:More Ground Crews.png|30px]]可以分配更多的地面人员，只要点击所需战略区域的图标即可。这将给战略区域内的任务效率带来{{绿}}+10%}}的奖励。
